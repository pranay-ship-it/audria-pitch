Why Field Sales First
A $7.2T market still running on gut instinct.
Companies like Rilla and Siro have proven the category: AI conversation intelligence for in-person sales teams (HVAC, roofing, solar, door-to-door). They've shown that analyzing field conversations drives 36-40% higher close rates. But they all share the same limitation: reps have to pull out their phone and hit record.
Their Problem
Manual recording means inconsistent capture. Reps forget, feel awkward pulling out a phone, or skip it entirely. Managers only see a fraction of conversations.
Audria's Edge
Arc is always on, always listening. 100% of conversations captured automatically with directional audio, speaker separation, and no phone needed. Zero friction.
Beyond Coaching
Audria doesn't just analyze after the fact. It acts: auto-generates follow-up emails, CRM updates, and calendar events. Real-time coaching whispered to the rep mid-conversation.
